Our Hiring Process

A 7-step process that delivers — every time

Every candidate flows through this proven chain — from understanding your organisation to monitoring performance long after joining.

01

Organisation Deep-Dive

We study your business, culture, org structure, and role requirements in detail. Great hiring starts with understanding you.

02

Strategic Hiring Plan

A customised recruitment strategy per role — including targeted job promotions across multiple platforms for maximum reach.

03

Grassroots Talent Sourcing

We tap our rural networks across Tamil Nadu, campus connections, referrals, job boards, and voluntary applications.

04

Assessment & Screening

Skills tests, psychometric profiling, communication assessment, and cultural-fit interviews weed out mismatches early.

05

Comprehensive Readiness

Selected candidates are prepared in life skills, communication, and role-specific competencies before deployment.

06

Job-Readiness Evaluation

Before deployment, every candidate is evaluated for readiness. Only job-ready candidates are presented to you.

07

Placement + Performance Monitoring

We place candidates and keep monitoring — regular check-ins with you and the candidate, with ongoing coaching for long-term retention.
