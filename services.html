Our Services

Complete white-collar hiring — at every level

Talent Expert is a white-collar recruitment firm serving SMEs and corporates across Tamil Nadu and India. We place professionals from high-potential graduates to CXO-level leaders — across 8 high-growth industries.

Get Talent Now See our process
What we do

Six services, one partner

Service 01

Mid-Management Placement
Overview

The backbone of every organisation is its middle-management layer. We identify, evaluate, and place experienced professionals in functional leadership roles — so your teams are led by people who deliver results.

10–15 day turnaround
RolesManagers, Senior Executives, Team Leaders, Department Heads, Assistant Managers
IndustriesIT, Manufacturing, Pharma, FMCG, Power/Solar, Textile, Real Estate, Services
ProcessCompetency-based screening, behavioural interviews, cultural-fit evaluation
Turnaround10–15 business days from requirement closure
Service 02

Senior Management Placement
Overview

Senior leaders shape strategy, culture, and outcomes. We go beyond résumé-matching — using deep industry knowledge and professional networks to identify leaders who transform teams and drive measurable growth.

Confidential search capability
RolesVice Presidents, General Managers, Senior Directors, Business Unit Heads, Regional Heads
  • Confidential search for sensitive, replacement, or succession hires
  • 360° evaluation — leadership competency, business acumen, stakeholder management
  • Reference verification & background screening included
Service 03

CXO & Executive Leadership Search
Overview

The most consequential hire your organisation makes is at the top. Our CXO practice brings a structured, discreet, and rigorous approach to C-suite and board-level search — connecting you with leaders who redefine what's possible.

Discreet, confidential outreach
RolesCEO, COO, CFO, CTO, CMO, CHRO, VP-level & Board Advisors
IndustriesAll 8 focus sectors — IT, Manufacturing, Pharma, FMCG, Power/Solar, Textile, Real Estate, Services
MethodMarket mapping, target-company intelligence, discreet outreach; dedicated industry-specific search team
Service 04

Fresher & Graduate Trainee Placement
Overview

Build your future leadership pipeline today. We identify high-potential graduates and prepare them in life skills, communication, and role-specific competencies before placing them in trainee programmes.

Psychometric profiling
SectorsIT, FMCG, Services, Pharma, Real Estate
  • Psychometric profiling to identify management potential
  • Life skills, communication & behavioural training included
  • Ideal for structured Management Trainee (MT) programmes
Service 05

Employee Training & Development
Overview

Your best investment is your existing team. Our bespoke corporate programmes sharpen functional skills, build leadership capability, and create a culture of continuous growth.

Tailored for SMEs & corporates
Programmes
  • Leadership development for mid & senior management
  • Functional-skills workshops — communication, presentation, strategic thinking
  • Performance-improvement & behavioural-change programmes
  • Customised training calendars designed for SMEs & corporates
Service 06

End-to-End Recruitment Management
Overview

For organisations without a dedicated talent-acquisition function, we become your on-demand HR partner — managing the entire hiring lifecycle with full transparency and accountability.

Your on-demand HR department
The lifecycle
  • Understand — your culture, structure & role requirements
  • Plan & promote — strategic hiring plan, targeted multi-platform outreach
  • Source & screen — candidate tracking, interview coordination
  • Close — offer management, negotiation, documentation, onboarding
  • Follow up — post-joining check-ins & performance reviews
