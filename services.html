Our Services

Complete white-collar hiring — at every level.

Talent Expert is a white-collar recruitment firm serving SMEs and corporates across Tamil Nadu and India. We place professionals from high-potential graduates to CXO-level leaders — across 8 high-growth industries.

Get Talent Now See our process
Service 01

Mid-Management Placement

The backbone of every organisation is its middle-management layer. We identify, evaluate, and place experienced professionals in functional leadership roles — so your teams are led by people who deliver results.

  • Roles: Managers, Senior Executives, Team Leaders, Department Heads, Assistant Managers
  • Industries: IT, Manufacturing, Pharma, FMCG, Power/Solar, Textile, Real Estate, Services
  • Process: Competency-based screening, behavioural interviews, cultural-fit evaluation
  • Turnaround: 10–15 business days from requirement closure
Service 02

Senior Management Placement

Senior leaders shape strategy, culture, and outcomes. We go beyond resume-matching — using deep industry knowledge and professional networks to identify leaders who transform teams and drive measurable growth.

  • Roles: VPs, General Managers, Senior Directors, Business Unit Heads, Regional Heads
  • Confidential search for sensitive, replacement, or succession hires
  • 360° evaluation: leadership competency, business acumen, stakeholder management
  • Reference verification and background screening included
Service 03

CXO & Executive Leadership Search

The most consequential hire your organisation makes is at the top. Our CXO practice brings a structured, discreet, and rigorous approach to C-suite and board-level search.

  • Roles: CEO, COO, CFO, CTO, CMO, CHRO, VP-level and Board Advisors
  • Industries: All 8 focus sectors
  • Bespoke methodology: market mapping, target-company intelligence, discreet outreach
  • Serving PE-backed businesses, fast-scaling SMEs, and established corporates
Service 04

Fresher & Graduate Trainee Placement

Build your future leadership pipeline today. We identify high-potential graduates and prepare them in life skills, communication, and role-specific competencies before placement.

  • White-collar fresher placement for IT, FMCG, Services, Pharma, Real Estate
  • Psychometric profiling to identify management potential
  • Life skills, communication, and behavioural readiness included
  • Ideal for structured Management Trainee (MT) programmes
Service 05

Employee Training & Development

Your best investment is your existing team. Our bespoke corporate programmes sharpen functional skills, build leadership capability, and create a culture of continuous growth.

  • Leadership development programmes for mid and senior management
  • Functional-skills workshops: communication, presentation, strategic thinking
  • Performance-improvement and behavioural-change programmes
  • Customised training calendars designed for SMEs and corporates
Service 06

End-to-End Recruitment Management

For organisations without a dedicated talent-acquisition function, we become your on-demand HR partner — managing the entire hiring lifecycle with full transparency and accountability.

  • Understanding your organisation: culture, structure, and role requirements
  • Strategic hiring plan and targeted multi-platform job promotion
  • Sourcing, screening, candidate tracking, interview coordination
  • Offer management, negotiation, documentation, and onboarding
  • Post-joining follow-up and performance check-ins

One partner for every level of hire.
